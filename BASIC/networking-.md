## **UNIT – I**

### **1) Define Computer Network, Goals & Applications**

**Computer Network:**  
A computer network is a collection of interconnected devices that share data and resources.

**Goals:**

- Resource sharing (printers, files)
    
- Communication
    
- Reliability
    
- Cost reduction
    

**Applications (Examples):**

- Email & Chat (communication)
    
- Online banking
    
- Cloud storage
    
- Video conferencing

### **3) Network Topologies**

**Topology:** Physical or logical arrangement of network devices.

**Types & Examples:**

- **Bus:** Single cable (old LANs)
    
- **Star:** Central switch (office LAN)
    
- **Ring:** Circular path
    
- **Mesh:** Multiple paths (internet backbone)
    
- **Tree:** Hierarchical network
