## OSWE Certification and Career Planning

  

#### **1. OSWE (Offensive Security Web Expert) Certification Details**

  

*   **Official Name:** OSWE is also listed as **WEB-300** on the Offensive Security website.

*   **Core Skill:** A strong understanding of **Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)** is essential, as it's a fundamental concept in all the required languages.

*   **Technical Requirements:** To pass the exam, proficiency is required in the following areas:

    *   **Programming Languages:**

        *   Java

        *   PHP

        *   .NET

        *   Python

    *   **Databases:**

        *   MySQL / MariaDB

        *   Oracle

        *   MSSQL

        *   PostgreSQL

*   **Exam Focus:**

    *   The curriculum covers advanced web attack vectors.

    *   A key requirement is the ability to perform **manual exploit development from scratch**. Relying on tools like ChatGPT for exploit generation is not a viable strategy for the exam.

  

#### **2. Pricing and Purchasing Strategy**

  

*   **Official Price:** The standard cost for both OSCP and OSWE is **$1749 USD**.

*   **Full Cost (India):** The price for OSCP is approximately **₹1.5 Lakh**, plus an additional **₹5,000 - ₹6,000** in hidden transaction fees.

*   **Cost-Saving Opportunity:**

    *   It's possible to purchase the certification for less through local, Delhi or Pune-based vendors who are OffSec partners.

    *   "Sachin sir" has a reference/connection with these vendors.

    *   Using these vendors can result in a saving of **₹25,000 - ₹30,000 INR**.

  

#### **3. Career and Certification Strategy**

  

*   **Initial Investment (OSCP):**

    *   It is recommended to pay for the **OSCP with your own money**.

    *   OSCP is highly valuable for securing a good entry-level job.

*   **Advanced Certifications (OSWE, etc.):**

    *   It is **not recommended** to pay for other advanced OffSec certifications with your own money.

    *   You should pursue these certifications only if a **company is willing to sponsor** the cost.

*   **Career Growth:**

    *   After approximately 2 years in a job, an expected salary is around 8 LPA.

    *   To achieve a significant salary jump to **~18 LPA**, the OSCP alone is not sufficient.

    *   Advancement requires a combination of **advanced certifications (like OSWE), strong communication skills, and deep technical knowledge.**

  

#### **4. Expected Salary Progression Benchmark**

  

This is a target career trajectory to aim for, where not meeting this pace is considered a "loss."

  

*   **1st Job:** ~5 LPA

*   **2nd Job:** ~10 LPA

*   **3rd Job:** ~15 LPA

*   **4th Job:** ~20 LPA

*   **5th Job:** ~25 LPA

*   *Goal after 3 years should be a salary greater than 18 LPA.*
